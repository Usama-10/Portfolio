·         OVERVIEW

iPhone is a chain of smartphones developed and manufactured by Apple Inc. these smartphones run on the Apple-owned iOS operating system. The first ever iPhone was launched in 2007 by the CEO of Apple Steve Jobs. Afterward, Apple releases new iPhone models and iOS updates each year. More than 2.2B iPhones have been sold till today.

·         IPHONE 14 SERIES

The latest is the iPhone 14 series launched on September 16, 2022. This series includes 4 models iPhone 14,iPhone 14 plus, iPhone 14 Pro, and iPhone 14 pro max available in unique and vibrant colors. Apple did away with the iPhone mini. As usual, the iPhone 14 series comes with awesome upgrades just like Apple does each year.

·         FEATURES

The iPhone 14 series has a pack full of great features, this year Apple introduced a notch which is called the dynamic island, eSIM, an always-on display, iOS 16 upgrade, A16 bionic chip, emergency SOS, car crash detection, satellite connectivity, action mode, screen brightness up to 2000 nits, photonic engine, up to 120Hz refresh rate and of course finally a 48MP camera setup.

The most headlined features of the iPhone 14 series are dynamic island notch, emergency SOS, car crash detection, and satellite connectivity.

·         PERFORMANCE

The iPhone 14 series is now equipped with an A16 Bionic chip (only in 14 pro and 14 pro max) which gives a 10% boost to performance and it is 20% more efficient than last year’s 15 bionic because of 4nm architecture.

Now the next comes is the cameras the front-facing camera doesn’t seem to be different but now it has autofocus. The games are changed when it comes to rear cameras. For the first time in the iPhone's history, the main camera is the 48MP sensor giving awesome shots in low light conditions, a 12MP ultrawide lens for outdoor views with an improved anti-distortion tech. The larger 1.4-micron pixels depth lens for improved macro photography to take impressively detailed shots. The last comes the 12MP telephoto lens equipped with 3x zoom ideal for awesome portrait shots.

When it comes to videography, there is a huge improvement iPhone can now support a 4k video recording at 60fps better than the previous model which has 30fps, with HDR and Dolby vision supported and an action mode that reduced camera shake.

Android devices are increasing battery performance, when it comes to apple it doesn’t seem to be the same. Apple never tells publicly the battery capacity in mAH. The company claims that the new iPhone 14 can last up to 23 hours while watching videos (1 hr more than the previous model) which is not a huge upgrade along with 25W fast charging (not included in the box), 15W MagSafe, and 7.5W Qi wireless charging.

·         PROS AND CONS

Improved camera and overall performance 10 times better than the previous version, the dynamic island seems to be very unique notch replacement, the full screen always on display, car crash detection, next level display quality.

No sim tray, no fast charging, not much battery improvement (even worse than last year), very expensive (especially in the UK), emergency SOS working only in USA and Canada.

 

 

·         WORTH BUYING OR NOT

The first factor is the prices, the normal iPhone 14 starts from $799 for the 128GB variant and ends at $1499 for the iPhone 14 pro max 1TB variant. Although in the UK, it is hard to recommend 1099 pounds for the pro version with a massive increase of 150 pounds from last year's variant. No SIM option in upper models would bother some audiences. The biggest mistake that attracts most of the audience is the battery performance. If we think of the android side, Apple is too far away from this competition.

The feature that attracts most of the audience is the stunning design and 10x better camera performance with fully loaded features. Despite these, there are no such upgrades that would lead you to buy or upgrade to iPhone 14 series.
